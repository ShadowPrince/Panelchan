Welcome to Panelchan!

This is main screen. Every series that you add end up here, sorted by newest. You can continue to read the series by touching it or edit/delete by holding it.

The last two buttons are always Add and Settings. With Add you can, no surprises, add another series to the list (adding is kind of an complicated process which will be discussed when you get there). Do I have to tell you what happens if you tap Settings? Everything there should be pretty self-explanatory.

The list itself consist of thumbnails, names and urls. URLs and thumbnails are updated automatically, titles can be edited on series Edit screen and default to page title. You can check settings to change automatic update behaviour (so you can make fancy thumbnails etc).

TL/DR: series sorted by newest; check settings for some advanced stuff.
